JL Mann Girls Lacrosse Team won the AAAAA State Championship on Saturday April 29, 2017 at Chapin High School beating Wando 9-8. This is the 6th State Championship for Head Coach Kristy Schumacher.

Seat Time Recovery is a program that allows students to recover time missed in classes due to too many absences. One hour of STR served equals one hour of class time recovered and students may earn back no more than a total of 60 hours in one year. Hours may not be earned before the absence occurs. Each 3-hour STR session costs $10 and payments are handled by the J. L. Mann Attendance Office. We ask that payments be made during lunch. There will be no refunds or partial payments. All sessions are from 3:55-6:15 p.m. in the J. L. Mann Media Center. See the form for the dates that seat time is held.

Students who participate in State and District testing and receive college-reportable scores on the ACT may view their scores and score report on a free ACT student web account. Scores are available online three to eight weeks after the test date. Scores from February are on a slight delay. However, ACT is working to upload scores by the end of April.
